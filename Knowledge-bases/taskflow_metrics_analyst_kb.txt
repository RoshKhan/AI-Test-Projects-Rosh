TOP 3 METRICS DRIVING BUSINESS RISK
-------------------------------------
1. Free-to-paid conversion: 8.4% vs 11.2% baseline
   Root cause: Workspace setup drop-off (-17.9pp) and
   mobile-only onboarding converting at 3.2% vs 22.4% CSM-assisted

2. Mobile DAU down 15%: 1,259 vs 1,481 baseline
   Root cause: v4.1.8 regression — crash rate 5.9x increase,
   push delay from 45s to 2.8 hours, offline sync at 61%

3. Monthly churn doubled: 1.6% vs 0.8% baseline
   Root cause: Mobile quality (34% of exit survey) +
   onboarding difficulty (22% of exit survey) = 56% combined

BRIGHT SPOTS
-------------
- MAU still growing: new signups strong
- Reporting and automation adoption increasing
- AI feature adoption at +8pp (fastest growing)
- Power user NRR at 91% retention
- Referral conversion at 13.1% (1.56x average)
- Enterprise retention at 72% M3 (healthy)

KEY CORRELATION
----------------
The mobile regression from v4.1.8 (April 2026) is the single
event most correlated with:
  - Mobile DAU decline acceleration (-6.1% in May after -7.4% April)
  - Support ticket surge in mobile category (+41% MoM)
  - NPS drop from 42 to 38 (April to May)
  - Conversion decline from 9.9% to 8.4% (March to May)
A stable mobile platform is the single highest-leverage fix
available to reverse all four metrics simultaneously.
